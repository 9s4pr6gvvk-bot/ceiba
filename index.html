About CEIBA

Rooted in experience. Focused on what's possible.

For years I worked in change management — developing people, motivating teams, helping organisations navigate uncertainty. Then my own life turned upside down.

And I discovered very quickly that real growth is nothing like a decision. It requires something far more demanding and far more rewarding — entering your internal world, digging deep, and uncovering what is actually there. The resources, the resilience, the strength you need are already inside you. They haven't gone. They've simply become hard to access.

That is where CEIBA was born. Not in a boardroom or a training manual — but through the hardest period of my own life, when I had to use everything I knew and everything I was to find my way through.

“Trust the process. The hard internal work will take time, commitment and truth — and it will bring you to a place of freedom to explore from a completely different landscape.”

I work with people who have lost their roots.

It can look like many things — a loss of identity, a career that no longer fits, a feeling of being stuck or restricted. Redundancy. Divorce. Empty nest. Starting a business. Limiting beliefs about what you're capable of. A quiet but persistent sense that something needs to change and you don't know where to begin.

What these experiences share is this: they shake the ground beneath you. And when the ground shifts, everything becomes harder — clarity, creativity, confidence, movement.

CEIBA exists for exactly that moment.

Built from 25 years of professional and lived experience — and grounded in a deep passion for helping others find theirs — CEIBA is coaching that doesn't tell you what to do. It helps you remember who you are.
