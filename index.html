About CEIBA

Rooted in experience.
Focused on what's possible.

For years I worked in change management — developing people, motivating teams, helping organisations navigate uncertainty. Then my own life turned upside down, and I discovered that real growth is nothing like a decision. It asks something far more demanding, and far more rewarding.

The resources, the resilience, the strength you need are already inside you. They haven't gone — they've simply become hard to access. That is where CEIBA was born.

ICF Qualified Coach · NLP Master Practitioner · Leadership, Change & Human Development

“
Trust the process. The hard internal work will take time, commitment and truth — and it will bring you to a place of freedom to explore from a completely different landscape.

I work with people who have lost their roots.

It can look like many things — a loss of identity, a career that no longer fits, a feeling of being stuck or restricted. Redundancy. Divorce. Empty nest. Starting a business. Limiting beliefs about what you're capable of. A quiet but persistent sense that something needs to change, and you don't know where to begin.

What these experiences share is this: they shake the ground beneath you. And when the ground shifts, everything becomes harder — clarity, creativity, confidence, movement. CEIBA exists for exactly that moment.

Built from 25 years of professional and lived experience — and grounded in a deep passion for helping others find theirs — CEIBA is coaching that doesn't tell you what to do. It helps you remember who you are.
