Questions

Things people often ask.

If you don't find what you're looking for here, get in touch — I'm happy to answer any question, no matter how small.

Send a question →

How long does coaching usually take?

−
There's no fixed number of sessions. Some clients find clarity in 6–8 sessions; others continue for much longer. We review progress together regularly and you decide the pace.

Do you work online or only in person?

+
Both. Sessions are available online, by telephone, or face to face — whichever works best for you and your schedule.

What's the difference between coaching and therapy?

+
Coaching is forward-focused — it helps you get clear on goals and take practical steps towards them. It isn't a substitute for therapy, and I'll always point you towards appropriate support if that's what's needed.

Is everything discussed confidential?

+
Yes. Coaching conversations are confidential. For organisational work, the scope of any reporting back is agreed clearly upfront with all parties.

Do you work with organisations as well as individuals?

+
Yes — alongside individual coaching, I work with leaders and teams on culture, psychological safety and organisational change through workshops and ongoing coaching.
